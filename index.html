ABOUT ME.

Hello, my name is Isaiah. I’m currently enrolled at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire with a major in Graphic Design. These past few years I have been practicing and improving my digital design skills in an attempt to better prepare myself in finding a job post graduation. I’ve always loved making art. Typically, my preferred mediums are anything related to sculpting and ceramics. I enjoy being ‘hands-on’ and being able to create something to the best of my abilities. Ever since I was a child I have been creating things. This has overall developed into a passion that has lead me to my career choice. My goal after graduating is to work for a company that I am passionate about and use my creativity effectively in designing for them.

When I’m neither at work or school, you can often find me outside. I love hiking in places I’ve never been and travelling. Every opportunity I get to experience something new, I take it. I feel like life is soslely made up of a list of opportunities; it’s only a matter of which ones we choose to take.
